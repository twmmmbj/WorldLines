# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want WorldLines spaces to feel constructive, curious, and safe.

Please:

- assume good faith and discuss disagreements calmly
- criticize ideas, implementations, and trade-offs — not people
- be respectful across languages, backgrounds, and experience levels
- give actionable feedback and keep review threads on topic

## Unacceptable Behavior

This includes, but is not limited to:

- harassment, intimidation, dogpiling, or personal attacks
- hate speech or demeaning language
- sexualized attention or unwelcome conduct
- doxxing or sharing private information without permission
- deliberately disruptive spam, trolling, or bad-faith escalation

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to this repository's issues, pull requests,
discussions, release notes, and other community spaces operated for
WorldLines.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates this
policy, and may limit participation when needed to protect the project
and its contributors.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness a serious problem, contact
`info@worldlines.gg` with relevant context or screenshots.

## Project-Specific Note

WorldLines is multilingual and spans public docs plus template content.
Please be especially careful when discussing translation quality,
creative direction, or product strategy: directness is welcome, contempt
is not.
