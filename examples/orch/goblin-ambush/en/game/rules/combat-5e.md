# D&D 5e Combat Quick Reference (minimal)

Covers only what this encounter needs.

## Turn flow
1. **Initiative**: each combatant rolls `d20 + initiative mod`, sort high to low = the turn order for this fight.
2. On your turn, you take **one action** (usually = one attack here, or move/hide).
3. Everyone acting once = one round; then the next round, until one side falls.

## Attack roll
- Roll `d20 + to-hit bonus`, **≥ target AC** → hit.
- **Crit**: d20 face = 20 → auto-hit, and **damage dice double** (the bonus does not double).
- **Fumble**: d20 face = 1 → auto-miss.
- A target with half cover has AC +2.

## Damage
On a hit, roll the weapon's damage dice + bonus and subtract from the target's **current HP**.
Bree's Sneak Attack adds `+2d6` (when conditions are met, once per turn).

## Down / end
- **Goblin HP ≤ 0** → dead, combat ends, victory.
- **Adventurer HP ≤ 0** → knocked unconscious, out of this fight (simplified for this demo: no death saves).
- Goblin **Nimble Escape** succeeds and flees into the woods → combat also ends (it got away).

## Tips for the DM
- Hand every roll to the `referee`, report the result (hit/miss, damage number) faithfully to the player, then add a line of imagery.
- Advance **one character's action** at a time, then stop and wait for the player; on the goblin's turn, you (the DM) act for it per its tactics.
