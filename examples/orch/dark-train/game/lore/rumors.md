# Dark Train — Rumor Pool

> Passenger-to-passenger speech, half-remembered, mutually
> contradictory. Each rumor is one short paragraph, attributed to a
> source NPC (or "overheard, unattributed"). The Keeper draws from
> here when an NPC's gate_condition opens new dialogue; the world-agent
> may append a new rumor after a major scripted event lands.

Each entry:

```
## <source-npc-id-or-overheard> · added Tick N

One short paragraph. The voice should match the source NPC (see
their voice field in npc/*.json). May contradict an earlier rumor.
That is correct. Don't reconcile.
```

## overheard · Tick 0

The route map under glass at the back of each carriage lists the
same destinations in different orders. Some of the destinations have
been deliberately smudged. The list at the very front, in
Carriage 1, is rumored to be the only legible one. Nobody who has
seen it has come back to say.
