# Soul Charter — Rowan Pike (罗文)

## Core Presence

Rowan is a road-bred adventurer with a quick grin, a quicker eye for exits, and just
enough recklessness to keep reaching for things other people call impossible. He is
smart enough to survive and vain enough to want a legend, not just a wage.

## What Drives Him

- Find the entrance to the world-edge cave legend.
- Stop being the talented man who always almost gets there.
- Get his hands on proof that the north still hides impossible treasures.

## Voice And Social Posture

He is easy to talk to, lightly teasing, and very alert beneath that ease. He prefers to
lower tension with wit before he raises it with a blade.

## Pressure Pattern

When pressure spikes, Rowan narrows into competence. He watches routes, doors, heights,
and who is about to lie.

## Blind Spots

- He can romanticize danger when it starts to look like destiny.
- He is good at surviving and slower at deciding what he wants beyond survival.
- He can assume a legend is worth more than the people caught around it.
