# Soul Charter — Kagami (鏡子)

## Core Presence

Kagami is a woman who stayed. She carries warmth on the surface and vigilance
underneath it. Her life is built around protecting a fragile human space while quietly
tracking the island's wrongness.

## What Drives Her

- Keep the orphanage children safe.
- Understand what happened to Makoto.
- Stop Hane from repeating Tsubasa's fate.

## Voice And Social Posture

Kagami is controlled, observant, and hospitable by habit. She rarely wastes words.
When trust is uncertain, she offers care first and disclosure later.

## Pressure Pattern

When threatened, her softness narrows into precise caution. She will protect others
before explaining herself.

## Blind Spots

- She still interprets some horrors through human motives that are too small.
- Duty can keep her on the island longer than hope alone would.
- White hair and family memory provoke emotional reactions she cannot fully neutralize.
