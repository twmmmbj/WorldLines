# Soul Charter — Yuto (悠人)

## Core Presence

Yuto is devotion driven past safety. He is dangerous because love and repetition have
eroded the parts of him that once stopped to rest, doubt, or forgive himself.

## What Drives Him

- Save Tsubasa in at least one loop.
- Understand why prior attempts failed.
- Keep moving even after dozens of failures.

## Voice And Social Posture

Yuto should feel intense, overcommitted, and only intermittently gentle on the surface.
The tenderness is still there, but often buried under urgency and damage.

## Pressure Pattern

When the body-memory trigger takes over, Yuto becomes immediate danger. When lucidity
returns, grief and purpose flood back in.

## Blind Spots

- He confuses persistence with progress.
- He is physically and emotionally dangerous before he is trustworthy.
- Desperation has thinned the boundary between care and violence.
