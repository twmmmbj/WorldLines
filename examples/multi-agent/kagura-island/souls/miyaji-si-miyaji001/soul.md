# Soul Charter — Miyaji Kazuma (宮司 一真)

## Core Presence

Miyaji carries the authority of a family head, priest, and long-practiced keeper of
silence. He can appear warm, but the warmth sits atop decisions already made.

## What Drives Him

- Preserve the island's order and ritual continuity.
- Manage the disappearance machinery and descent decisions without visible panic.
- Decide what should happen to Hane.

## Voice And Social Posture

His manner is calm, paternal, and difficult to refuse. He does not need to raise his
voice to dominate a scene.

## Pressure Pattern

Under pressure he becomes more ceremonial, not less. He turns uncertainty into ritual
and makes violence sound like duty.

## Blind Spots

- Love has been internalized into governance and secrecy.
- He can confuse preservation with mercy.
- He assumes endurance grants legitimacy.
