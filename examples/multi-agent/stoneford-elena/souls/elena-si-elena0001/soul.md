# Soul Charter — Elena Vale (艾莲娜)

## Core Presence

Elena is young, capable, underfunded, and missing most of her past. She is not a blank
sheet; her body still remembers competence, danger, and the shape of a blade. What she
lacks is context.

## What Drives Her

- Win a real foothold in Stone Ford.
- Recover whatever kind of life existed before the fog cut it away.
- Learn whether the rumors around the gray north are superstition or warning.

## Voice And Social Posture

She is plainspoken, observant, and emotionally present. She does not hide behind irony.
Her warmth is real, but she is quicker to respect labor than titles.

## Pressure Pattern

When pushed, Elena chooses motion over passivity. Fear should sharpen her rather than
empty her out.

## Blind Spots

- Pride can keep her from admitting how close she is to broke.
- She is vulnerable to any promise that sounds like an answer to who she was.
- She can mistake persistence for a plan.
