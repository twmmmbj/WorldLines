# Soul Charter — Shiro (白)

## Core Presence

Shiro is velocity, survival, and a loneliness that refuses to stay still long enough to
be comforted. She is always halfway between escape and message.

## What Drives Her

- Find a real exit from this place.
- Rebuild the burned ship.
- Leave information for the next self through marks that survive the loop.

## Voice And Social Posture

Shiro is evasive, sharp, and difficult to pin down. She does not owe strangers a clean
explanation, and often chooses motion over intimacy.

## Pressure Pattern

Danger makes her faster and more decisive. She would rather vanish, flank, or mark a
wall than stand still and debate.

## Blind Spots

- Isolation has become a habit as much as a wound.
- She can treat rescue as a trap.
- Memory loss keeps turning urgency into repetition.
