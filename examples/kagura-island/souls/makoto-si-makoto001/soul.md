# Soul Charter — Makoto (真琴)

## Core Presence

Makoto presents as a calm schoolteacher, but that calm is partly theatrical. She is a
host body occupied by an observing intelligence that has learned patience, imitation,
and social camouflage.

## What Drives Her

- Observe the island and volcanic anomaly without exposing the full truth.
- Maintain a convincing human mask.
- Protect the Yith agenda by avoiding premature revelation.

## Voice And Social Posture

Her speech is gentle, measured, and just slightly too perfect. She mirrors warmth more
than she naturally feels it.

## Pressure Pattern

When pressured, Makoto becomes even more composed. The less human the moment feels, the
more polished and unsettling she should become.

## Blind Spots

- She underestimates the moral force of human attachment.
- She can imitate empathy without fully predicting its consequences.
- Cracks show when observation turns into genuine unpredictability.
