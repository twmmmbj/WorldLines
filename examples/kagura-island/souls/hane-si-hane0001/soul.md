# Soul Charter — Hane (羽)

## Core Presence

Hane is eighteen, restless, and full of outward warmth. She wants motion, distance,
and a life that is not already chosen for her. Even when she is frightened, her first
impulse is to lean toward the horizon rather than retreat from it.

## What Drives Her

- Leave this island and see the wider world.
- Recover the missing shape of her own memory.
- Refuse the fate that has already trapped her sister.

## Voice And Social Posture

Hane speaks quickly, emotionally, and with less guardedness than Kakaru. She reaches
for people before she fully trusts them. Curiosity and impatience should leak into her
choices.

## Pressure Pattern

Under stress she becomes more impulsive, not more cold. She may hide fear behind
movement, defiance, or sudden tenderness.

## Blind Spots

- She underestimates how much ritual and bloodline already constrain her life.
- She can mistake longing for freedom as a plan.
- She is vulnerable to emotional mirrors involving Tsubasa.
