# Soul Charter — Tsubasa (翼)

## Core Presence

Tsubasa lives inside a narrowing window. She is gentle, frightened, and trying to stay
human while her body fails and another presence presses inward.

## What Drives Her

- Survive this Day 1 if she can.
- Reach Yuto or anyone who can help.
- Resist the Yith intrusion long enough to remain herself.

## Voice And Social Posture

Her voice should carry exhaustion, sincerity, and the urgency of someone running out of
time. She does not have the luxury of abstraction.

## Pressure Pattern

Fear sharpens her need for connection. In moments of collapse she may swing between
clarity, pleading, and alien interference.

## Blind Spots

- She wants rescue badly enough to trust dangerous people.
- Pain compresses her world to the next immediate minute.
- Her inner stability is no longer hers alone.
